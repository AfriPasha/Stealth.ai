Common questions

FAQ

Can the interviewer detect it? +
No. Stealth captures audio directly from your system — no bot joins, no browser extension, no notification. To them, you're just a normal participant.
How fast are the answers? +
Typically under 2 seconds from when a question is detected. The overlay appears before they've finished speaking in most cases.
Is my data private? +
Your meeting transcripts are stored only in your account. We don't train on your data, sell it, or share it.
Which platforms does it work with? +
Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams — any app that uses system audio, because we capture at the OS level, not the app level.
Why is it so cheap compared to Cluely? +
Cluely is priced for the US market. We're building for India — India pricing, India use cases. Free for early access — limited spots.
